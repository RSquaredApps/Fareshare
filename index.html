Section 1

Privacy Policy

RSquared Apps respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Fareshare Tip Calculator handles information when you use the iOS mobile application.

Fareshare Tip Calculator is designed as a private, on-device bill splitting and tipping tool. RSquared Apps does not collect, store on its servers, sell, rent, share, or transmit user personal data.

Data Collection

Fareshare Tip Calculator does not collect personal data from users.

  • Fareshare Tip Calculator does not require a user account.
  • Fareshare Tip Calculator does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, receipt images, identifiers, analytics data, advertising identifiers, or usage tracking data.
  • Fareshare Tip Calculator does not transmit user data to RSquared Apps.
  • Fareshare Tip Calculator does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or crash-reporting services.

Local App Data

Fareshare Tip Calculator may save app settings and app-entered information locally on your device so the app can function. This may include:

  • Bill totals, tax amounts, tip percentages, and split modes
  • Receipt item names and prices that you enter or edit
  • People names that you enter for splitting a bill
  • Item assignments used for itemized splitting
  • Country, currency, and tipping custom preferences
  • Recently calculated split summaries

This information is stored locally on your device. RSquared Apps does not receive it, access it, or transmit it to any external server.

Camera and Receipt Scanning

Fareshare Tip Calculator may request camera access so you can scan a restaurant receipt.

  • Receipt scanning is optional.
  • Receipt text recognition is handled on device using Apple system frameworks where available.
  • RSquared Apps does not receive, upload, store, or review your receipt images.
  • You may deny or disable camera permission at any time in iOS Settings.

If you do not use receipt scanning, Fareshare Tip Calculator does not use the camera.

Contacts and Sharing

Fareshare Tip Calculator may offer a feature that lets you choose a contact and share a split summary by text message.

  • Contacts access is optional and used only when you choose to select a recipient.
  • RSquared Apps does not receive, store, or transmit your contacts.
  • Fareshare Tip Calculator does not upload contact information to any server.
  • Message sending is controlled by Apple Messages and your device settings.
  • You may disable Contacts permission at any time in iOS Settings.

Currency and Tipping Guidance

Fareshare Tip Calculator includes country, currency, and tipping custom information to help travelers estimate tips and split bills.

This guidance is informational only. Exchange rates, local customs, restaurant policies, service charges, and taxes may vary. Always review your receipt and local requirements before paying.

Data Retention

Because RSquared Apps does not collect or store user personal data on its servers, RSquared Apps does not retain user personal data.

Any app information saved locally on your device remains there until you:

  • Change or clear it in the app, where available
  • Delete the Fareshare Tip Calculator app from your device
  • Reset or erase your device according to Apple iOS settings

Device backups, if enabled by the user through Apple services, are controlled by Apple and the user’s device settings, not by RSquared Apps.

Third-Party Processing

Fareshare Tip Calculator does not use third-party services for analytics, advertising, tracking, data processing, or cloud storage.

No third party receives user personal data from RSquared Apps through Fareshare Tip Calculator.

Data Sharing

RSquared Apps does not sell, rent, trade, share, or disclose Fareshare Tip Calculator user personal data because RSquared Apps does not collect that data.

User Rights and Choices

Because Fareshare Tip Calculator does not require an account and RSquared Apps does not collect user personal data, most privacy choices are controlled directly on your device.

  • Clear bill, receipt, or split information in Fareshare Tip Calculator, where available
  • Disable camera or contacts access in iOS Settings
  • Delete the app to remove locally stored app data from the device
  • Contact RSquared Apps at rsquaredsupport@gmail.com with privacy questions

If you contact RSquared Apps by email, your email address and message will be used only to respond to your request.

Children’s Privacy

Fareshare Tip Calculator is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

RSquared Apps does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to RSquared Apps, contact rsquaredsupport@gmail.com, and RSquared Apps will take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

RSquared Apps may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
